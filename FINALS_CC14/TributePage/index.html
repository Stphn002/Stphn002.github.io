Leonardo da Vinci

My Personal Art Hero

Why I Look Up at Him:

Growing up, I was really fond of everything related to arts. I learned how to draw, paint, and write at the age of 5. As years passed by, I became more and more obsessed with drawing that I even tried different art styles:

  • Japanese Anime
  • Western Cartoon
  • Architectural Buildings
  • Realism Portraits
  • Landscape and Seascape Painting
  • Animation 2D and 3D

    • Even though I tried these different styles, his personal drawing style was stucked deep inside me. When I was around 7, our family had a book about him, and inside the book were drawings and paintings that he created. From that day, I always tried to copy his style yet my skill is miles away from him.

    He is my personal inspiration on why up until now, I still continue my passion in arts.
